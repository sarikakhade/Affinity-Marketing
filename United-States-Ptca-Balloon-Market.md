Application II: Stent Deployment Assistance Application II of the PTCA balloon market relates to the deployment of stents during angioplasty procedures. This combined approach of balloon angioplasty and stent deployment is often used to treat more severe forms of coronary artery disease and is a standard procedure in the field of interventional cardiology. The shift toward biodegradable technologies is likely to increase as research in this area progresses and patient safety continues to be a priority in cardiovascular procedures.
